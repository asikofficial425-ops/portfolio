01 / About

Background & goals

I'm a self-taught graphic designer and computer science student who loves turning a blank canvas into something people actually want to look at — and, increasingly, building it myself too. Over the past couple of years I've balanced freelance design work with my degree, and I'm chasing the same goal in both worlds.

My focus right now is getting better at the handoff between design and code — designing interfaces in Figma and then actually shipping them, clean and responsive, in the browser. I care about clarity over decoration, and I like projects where both sides matter.
